# Validation matrix

This page maps the library's published claims to bounded evidence. It is a
review map, not a replacement for the tests, diagnostics pages, notebooks, or
benchmark artifacts themselves.

| Area | Claim being made | Evidence type | Where to review | Caveat |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
